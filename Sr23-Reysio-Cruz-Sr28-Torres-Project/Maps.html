Maps

There are 7 Maps in Valorant. Namely, Ascent, Bind, Breeze, Haven, Icebox, Pearl and Split. There are often certain aspects that you can interact with in the map that make it different from other maps.



Ascent

To the left is the map of Ascent. The aspect that makes this different are the breakable doors that are seperating A Link/Tree and A Site, and B Site and Mid Market. It also has a breakble window that seperates A Heaven from A Link/Tree.


Bind

To the left is the map of Bind. The aspect that makes this map different is the teleport which can teleport you from A Port to B Short, and B Port to A Lobby.

Breeze

To the left is the map of Breeze. The aspect that makes this map different is the unbreakable door that seperates A hall from A site and a chute that will drop you from A hall to Mid.

Haven

To the left is the map of Haven. The aspect that makes this different is that it has 3 sites and the entrance to Garage is breakable.

Icebox

To the left is the map of Icebox. The aspect that makes this different is that it is the first map to introduce horizontal ropes and sites that have 2-story nests where you can also plant the spike on.

Pearl

To the left is the map of Pearl. The aspect that makes this different is that it is dogwater!

Split

To the left is the map of Split. The aspect that makes this different is it was the first one to introduce ropes, it is very secluded and mid directly connects to the 2 heavens of each site.
