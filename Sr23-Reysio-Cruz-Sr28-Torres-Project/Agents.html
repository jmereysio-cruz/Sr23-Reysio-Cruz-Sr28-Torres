Duelists

Duelists are expected to be self-sufficient fraggers who use their utility and bodies to create space and gain man-advantages for the team. Duelists need to be fearless.

Controllers

Controllers are expected to create advantages on the map for their team using their abilities. Timing and communication are two essential skills for controllers.

Initiators

Sentinels are defensive monsters who are expected to lock down sites for their team and prevent enemies from planting. Sentinels must be patient and focused.
